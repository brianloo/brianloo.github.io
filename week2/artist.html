Alejandro Saab

Alejandro Saab also known as Cyyu on the internet, is a talented voice actor known for his work in various Anime Shows and Video Games. With a distinctive voice and a wide range of characters, he has garnered a significant fanbase not only for his voice acting but also of his humor when live streaming. Some of his notable roles include Cyno in "Genshin Impact", Jing Yuan in "Honaki Star Rail" and Anton in "Zenless Zone Zero". His performances are praised for their emotional depth and versatility, making him a standout figure in the voice acting community.
